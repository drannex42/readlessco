Ghost Install -

## TITLES

The "headlines" are the title of the post
the "sources" are the body of the post 
    In the hbs you make it show the {{excepts-200}} so it shows all the sources. 
    
    ex hbs : 
        source: {{except}}
        
    in the body of the post :
        (cnn)[cnn.com], (yahoo)[yahoo.com]
        
## SECTIONS 

You use the "tag" section. 

Rewrite the tag code using to change it

    ex : 
        /tag/science to /science
        
Tutorial:
http://andy.stanton.is/writing/about/custom-post-tag-paths-with-ghost/
